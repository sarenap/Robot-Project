What pid values did you choose and why?

Loop:

Speed: 63
P: 33
I & D: 0
This is the optimal speed for our design to run loops quick enough while not shooting off the track. Going faster makes it harder to read the track accurately.

Frequency:

Speed: 17
P: 35-40
I & D: 0
The track has more sharp turns so we lower the speed and increase the P value to read the track accurately.

Drag Race:

Speed: 63
P: 33
I & D: 0
We used max speed since we are going in a straight line as quick as possible. Other values stayed the same.
